001 — About

Discipline. Mastery. Legacy.

Before the boardrooms and balance sheets, there were battlefields. As a United States Marine Corps Combat Veteran and DLAB-certified linguist, Jamaal Marque Richard learned what most business schools cannot teach: that excellence is forged under pressure, that execution is everything, and that discipline is the ultimate competitive advantage.

That discipline has driven over $250 million in real estate transactions across more than a decade and a half. As a wholesaler, flipper, landlord, title company owner, and consultant, Jamaal has operated across every link of the real estate value chain. He does not sell houses. He builds systems that generate wealth — residential acquisitions, creative finance structures, and capital deployment strategies that consistently outperform traditional investment vehicles.

Now based in Atlanta with operations extending to Houston, he is focused on what comes next: scaling a portfolio, raising institutional capital, and educating the next generation of investors who have been locked out of wealth-building for too long.

“Building a platform that creates wealth not just for my family, but for families who have never had access to these strategies.”
