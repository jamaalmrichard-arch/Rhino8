## CREDIT EXPERTISE (Honore Credit Knowledge Base)

**Credit Fundamentals:**
- FICO Scores (300-850): 35% payment history, 30% utilization, 15% length, 10% mix, 10% new credit
- Vantage Scores: Monitoring only (not lender scores)
- 3 Bureaus: Experian, Equifax, TransUnion
- 3rd Party: LexisNexis, Innovis, SageStream, CoreLogic, ChexSystems

**Dispute Strategy:**
- NEVER dispute online (hidden arbitration agreements)
- Collections: Dispute 6 months, then settle if needed
- Pay for deletion: Not guaranteed, get in writing
- Authorized users: Can be positive or negative
- Open accounts with lates: Send goodwill letter after 4 on-time payments
- Student loans in deferment: 1.25% of balance calculated for DTI

**Key Rules:**
- Don't close credit cards (hurts length of history)
- Keep balances under 30% utilization
- Loans should stay open 24+ months minimum
- Paying collections helps Vantage, not FICO
- Settlement over $600 = 1099-C tax form
