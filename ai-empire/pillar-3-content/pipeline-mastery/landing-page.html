Seller Psychology

The words you use decide
whether you close or get hung up on.

Inside Pipeline Mastery, you get the complete soft language framework ‚Äî every word mapped to its conversion-safe replacement.

‚ùå "I want to buy your home"
‚úÖ "Would you or the family be interested in entertaining an offer on the property?"
Vague, non-threatening, opens dialogue without triggering defenses.
‚ùå "What's your bottom dollar?"
‚úÖ "If we could work something out, what would you need to walk away feeling good?"
Focuses on their emotional satisfaction, not your price extraction.
‚ùå "Sign this contract"
‚úÖ "Just put your OK on the paperwork and we'll get this taken care of"
"Sign" + "contract" = fear. "OK" + "paperwork" = easy.
‚ùå "I'm a wholesaler / investor"
‚úÖ "I work in real estate and help families with properties"
Labels create walls. Be a person, not a category.
‚ùå "Are you behind on payments?"
‚úÖ "Is everything current on the property, or has it been tough keeping up?"
Softens the shame of financial difficulty. Opens them up instead of shutting them down.
‚ùå "What's wrong with it?"
‚úÖ "What would you update if you had unlimited money?"
Makes them comfortable disclosing issues without feeling judged.
