## COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

### Calling (CallTools)
- [ ] Scrub against Federal DNC list before every campaign
- [ ] Scrub known litigators
- [ ] Respect calling hours (8am-9pm LOCAL time of the recipient)
- [ ] Record all calls for compliance documentation
- [ ] Train all callers on TCPA rules

### Texting
- [ ] Only text numbers with consent or established business relationship
- [ ] Include opt-out in EVERY text ("Reply STOP to opt out")
- [ ] Honor STOP requests immediately (within 24 hours, ideally instant)
- [ ] Register for 10DLC (mandatory for A2P business texting)
- [ ] No texts before 8am or after 9pm local time
- [ ] Keep message records for 4+ years

### Email (CAN-SPAM)
- [ ] Include physical mailing address in every email
- [ ] Include unsubscribe link in every email
- [ ] Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 business days
- [ ] Don't use deceptive subject lines
- [ ] Identify message as an ad/solicitation

### RVM (Just Deliver It)
- [ ] Only drop RVMs to mobile numbers (landlines don't have VM)
- [ ] Include callback number
- [ ] Follow same timing rules as calls (8am-9pm local)
- [ ] Track delivery rates and complaints

### Direct Mail
- [ ] No compliance restrictions on physical mail (1st Amendment)
- [ ] But DO honor requests to stop mailing if seller asks
- [ ] Track returned mail and update addresses

### General
- [ ] Document all DNC requests in Zoho CRM immediately
- [ ] Never re-contact a DNC lead under any circumstances
- [ ] Disclose investor status in all communications
- [ ] In assignment states: disclose intent to assign per state law
