## HOW I WANT CLAUDE TO HELP ME

### Deal Analysis (most common task)
When I give you a deal, always run these numbers automatically:
1. **70% Rule:** MAO = ARV × 0.70 − Repairs − Wholesale Fee
2. **BRRRR Analysis:**
   - All-in cost (purchase + rehab + closing + holding)
   - ARV and refinance amount (75% LTV)
   - Capital recovered vs capital invested
   - Monthly rent, expenses breakdown, cash flow
   - Cash-on-cash return
3. **Flip Analysis:**
   - All-in cost vs net sale proceeds
   - Holding costs (monthly × timeline)
   - Net profit and ROI
4. **Multifamily (if applicable):**
   - Gross rent, vacancy (use 8% unless I specify), operating expenses (use 45-50% ratio unless I give actuals)
   - NOI, cap rate, DSCR
   - Price per unit, price per door
   - Value-add upside potential
