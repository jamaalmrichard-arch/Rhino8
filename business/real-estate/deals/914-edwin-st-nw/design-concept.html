Materials & Finishes

Quartz
White Quartz with Gold Veining
Countertops throughout kitchen and bathrooms. Calacatta-inspired pattern with warm gold veining on bright white base. 3cm thick with eased edge profile.
LVP
Wide Plank LVP — Light Oak
7" wide plank luxury vinyl plank in warm light oak tone. Waterproof, scratch-resistant. Installed throughout first and second floors. Wood-grain texture with matte finish.
Matte Black
Matte Black Appliances
Full suite: refrigerator, range, dishwasher, microwave. Black stainless or matte black finish. Fingerprint-resistant coating. Modern flat-panel design.
Brushed Gold
Brushed Gold Fixtures
All faucets, cabinet pulls, towel bars, light fixtures, door handles, and shower hardware in coordinated brushed/satin gold. Warm tone, not polished brass.
Large Format Tile — B/W
24"x24" matte porcelain tile in bathrooms. Black and white palette with geometric patterns. Floor-to-ceiling in master shower. Gold accent trim strips.
Shaker Cabinets — White
Soft-close shaker-style cabinets in bright white. Upper and lower kitchen cabinets with brushed gold bar pulls. 42" uppers to maximize storage.
Electric Fireplace Insert
50" recessed linear electric fireplace. Realistic flame effect with adjustable color. Black steel surround with gold trim frame. Focal wall in living room with floor-to-ceiling stone-look tile.
Accent
Royal Purple Accent Walls
Deep matte purple paint on feature walls in master bedroom (headboard wall) and office/bedroom 3. Creates drama against white walls and gold fixtures. Benjamin Moore "Gentle Violet" or similar.
