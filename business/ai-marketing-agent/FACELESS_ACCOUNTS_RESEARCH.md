## WHAT'S WORKING RIGHT NOW (2025-2026)

### Algorithm Changes — Critical Updates

**TikTok 2026 Algorithm:**
- **70%+ completion rate needed to go viral** (up from 50% in 2024)
- Follower engagement is now central — video shows to followers FIRST, then expands if it performs
- Rewatch rate over 15-20% = massive algorithmic boost
- One viewer watching 3x beats three viewers watching 1x
- **Hashtags:** Use hyper-specific niche tags, NOT #viral or #fyp (algorithm now uses categorical targeting)
- Quality over quantity — 1 high-retention video every 3 days beats 3 low-quality daily posts
- First 0.5-1 second determines 70-90% of viewer retention

**YouTube 2026 Algorithm:**
- Watch time and session time are king
- Finance content gets premium distribution due to advertiser demand
- Shorts feed now drives significant subscriber growth
- Evergreen content compounds views over months/years

**Instagram 2026 Algorithm:**
- Reels get 2x the reach of any other format
- Carousel engagement signals (saves, shares) heavily weighted
- Consistent posting schedule matters more than volume

### Viral Hook Strategies (What's Working NOW)

1. **Pattern Interrupt:** Open with silence in a platform dominated by music (40% higher completion rates)
2. **Controversial Statement:** "Why saving money is making you poor" — forces engagement
3. **Number Hook:** "I made $47,000 in 30 days doing this one thing"
4. **Question Hook:** "Why don't they teach this in school?"
5. **Urgency Hook:** "This loophole closes in 2026"
6. **Story Hook:** "I went from $200K in debt to owning 15 rentals"
7. **Contrast Hook:** Raw voiceover intensity + boring/mundane visuals = keeps users hooked
8. **Curiosity Gap:** "The #1 thing millionaires do that nobody talks about..."
