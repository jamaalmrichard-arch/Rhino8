What if I do not have $100K in retirement accounts?
The strategies in this guide work from $25,000 up. The math scales. If you have a smaller balance, Module 3 (Cash Flow Replacement Math) shows you the exact path from where you are to where you want to be — including how to grow your position over time.
Will this upset my financial advisor?
Probably. And that tells you everything you need to know. When you move capital into a Self-Directed IRA, it leaves your advisor’s fee ecosystem. They lose assets under management. This guide gives you the information to make your own decision about whether that is the right move for your situation.
Is this legal?
100% legal. Self-Directed IRAs have been IRS-approved since 1974. The Employee Retirement Income Security Act (ERISA) explicitly allows retirement funds to be invested in alternative assets, including real estate. Module 2 covers the legal framework, prohibited transactions, and compliance requirements in detail.
Can I do this without quitting my job?
That is the entire point. The Wealth Shift is designed for professionals who want to build passive income alongside their W-2. Module 4 covers how to evaluate deals in 30 minutes and manage your portfolio in 2–3 hours per month. You do not need to become a full-time landlord.

The trilogy teaches you how money works. The Wealth Shift teaches you what to do about it. This is a strategy guide with worksheets, calculators, audio briefings, and a 6-month execution plan. It is the bridge between understanding the system and repositioning your capital within it.
