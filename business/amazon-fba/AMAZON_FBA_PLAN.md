# Amazon — Digital Products & Affiliate Business Plan
## Priority: HIGH — Passive Revenue Generator
## Created: 2026-03-05
## Updated: 2026-03-05 — DIGITAL ONLY (no buy/resell physical products)

---

## THE PLAY

Sell digital products and push other people's products for commissions on Amazon. No inventory, no shipping, no physical products. Pure digital + affiliate.

## STRATEGY: Dual Track ($0-$50 start)

### Track A: Amazon KDP — Publish & Profit ($0 cost)
Publish books, journals, workbooks, planners, puzzle books on Amazon. 35-70% royalties. Zero inventory. Passive income. Can also republish Money Decoded content.

### Track B: Amazon Associates / Affiliate ($0 cost)
Recommend products through content (YouTube, TikTok, blog). Earn 1-10% commission per sale. Build content once, earn forever.

---

## 90-DAY SPRINT

### Week 1-2: Foundation ($0)
- [ ] Open Amazon Individual Seller Account (free, $0.99/sale)
- [ ] Install Keepa browser extension (free) — price history tracking
- [ ] Install SellerAmp SAS ($16.99/mo) — profit calculator
- [ ] Study Amazon Best Sellers list 2-3 hours
- [ ] Sign up for deal alerts: Slickdeals, CamelCamelCamel
- [ ] Start KDP: Create Canva account, design first 2 journals

### Week 2-4: First Purchases ($300)
- [ ] Buy 10-15 products, 2-5 units each from clearance sites
- [ ] Criteria: min $3 profit/unit, min 30% ROI, sales rank < 100,000
- [ ] Sources: Walmart.com clearance, Target.com, HomeDepot.com, Kohls.com
- [ ] Ship to Amazon FBA (or start with Merchant Fulfilled)
- [ ] Publish 4-8 KDP books (journals, planners, puzzle books)
- [ ] Expected: $300 → $390-$450 in revenue ($90-$150 profit)

### Week 4-8: Reinvest + Scale
- [ ] Reinvest ALL profits into inventory
- [ ] Build "winners list" — replenishable products
- [ ] Track every purchase in spreadsheet
- [ ] Continue KDP: 2-4 books/week
- [ ] Start exploring wholesale accounts with brands

### Week 8-12: Hit $1,000/Month
- [ ] $800-$1,200 in rotating inventory
- [ ] Upgrade to Professional account ($39.99/mo) at 40+ items/month
- [ ] Open 3-5 wholesale accounts with brands
- [ ] Apply for ungating in restricted categories
- [ ] KDP target: $100-$300/month passive

## DO NOT DO
- Pay $20K-$75K for "done-for-you" Amazon automation (FTC is suing these companies)
- Buy expensive courses (YouTube + Reddit + Amazon Seller University is free)
- Start with wholesale at $500 (need $2K+ for wholesale, use arbitrage first)
