## COURSE CURRICULUM (8 Modules)

### MODULE 1: The Mindset & The Machine
- Why 95% of wholesalers fail (and how to be the 5%)
- The Pipeline Mastery™ philosophy: systems > hustle
- Overview of the complete tech stack
- Setting your income goal and reverse-engineering the numbers
- Time audit: where your hours should actually go

### MODULE 2: Data & List Building Mastery
- PropStream deep dive — every filter, every list type explained
- The motivated seller hierarchy (which lists convert highest)
- List stacking — how to find the "golden leads" on 2-3+ lists
- Skip tracing with DataZapp — step by step
- Data hygiene: cleaning lists, removing duplicates, DNC scrubbing
- How often to pull new lists and refresh old ones

### MODULE 3: Your CRM is Your Business
- Zoho CRM setup walkthrough (step by step)
- Pipeline stages: from New Lead to Closed or Nurture
- Lead scoring model: auto-prioritize your hottest leads
- Automation workflows: tasks, emails, stage changes, alerts
- Reporting and dashboards: know your numbers at a glance
- Why most people use their CRM wrong (and how to fix it)

### MODULE 4: The 13-Touch Follow-Up System
- The psychology behind multi-channel follow-up
- Why most deals close on touch 5-12 (not touch 1)
- The complete 13-touch sequence: channel, timing, script, purpose
- Setting up CallTools for cold calling (campaigns, scripts, recordings)
- Setting up Just Deliver It for RVM drops
- Setting up text campaigns (platform selection, compliance, templates)
- Setting up email drip campaigns (Zoho Campaigns or alternative)
- Setting up direct mail (provider selection, postcard vs. letter, handwritten)
- Automation: how to trigger each touch from your CRM
- The monthly nurture system (for leads that don't convert in 55 days)

### MODULE 5: Seller Psychology & The Art of the Conversation
- The words that kill deals vs. the words that close deals (full soft language guide)
- The 4 Pillars of Pre-Qualifying (Condition, Timeline, Motivation, Price)
- 10 psychological principles that make sellers say yes
- Rapport building: on the phone and at the property
- Objection handling: 9 scripts for the most common objections
- Tonality, pacing, silence, and the power of listening
- Role-play exercises (practice with the group)

### MODULE 6: Making Offers & Getting Properties Under Contract
- Running the numbers: ARV, MAO, 70% rule, rehab estimates
- Structuring wholesale offers (assignment vs. double close)
- Structuring creative offers (subject-to, seller finance, wrap)
- Presenting offers without sounding like a predator
- Contract walkthrough: PSA, assignment, disclosures
- Handling the "your offer is too low" conversation
- Getting the paperwork signed at the kitchen table
- The assumptive close, the puppy dog close, the either/or close

### MODULE 7: Disposition — Getting Your Deals Sold
- Building your cash buyer list (50+ active buyers before you market)
- Buyer avatar: who buys what, where, and at what price
- How to market your deals to buyers (email blasts, groups, agent referrals)
- Pricing your assignment fee: too high kills the deal, too low leaves money on the table
- Managing the closing process with your title company
- Double close walkthrough (when to use it and how)
- For agents: how to refer wholesale deals for a fee or co-wholesale

### MODULE 8: Scaling & Building a Team
- Hiring your first VA (where to find them, what to pay, how to train)
- Hiring an acquisition manager (compensation structures)
- Hiring a disposition manager
- KPI tracking: what to measure, how often, and what the benchmarks are
- When to increase marketing spend (and when to pull back)
- Systemizing so the business runs without you
- Building a brand and generating inbound leads
- Packaging your expertise: coaching, co-wholesaling, JVs
- The path from wholesaling to owning 100+ rentals

## MARKETING & LAUNCH PLAN

### Lead Magnet (Free → Email List)
**"The Wholesaler's Cheat Sheet: 47 Words That Kill Deals (And What to Say Instead)"**
- PDF download, captures email
- Drives to webinar or VSL

### Webinar / VSL (Free → Application)
**"How I Built a 13-Touch Follow-Up System That Closes 4-6 Wholesale Deals/Month on Autopilot"**
- 45-60 min live or evergreen webinar
- Teaches the WHAT and WHY (not the HOW — that's in the course)
- Ends with CTA to apply for the program

### Content Engine (Ongoing → Warm Traffic)
- YouTube: 2-3 videos/week (deal breakdowns, script examples, psychology tips, tool tutorials)
- Instagram/TikTok: Short-form clips from YouTube content
- Podcast: Weekly episodes with deal reviews and guest experts
- Email: 2-3 emails/week to list (value + soft pitch)

### Paid Ads (Scale → Cold Traffic)
- Facebook/Instagram: Webinar registration ads
- YouTube: Pre-roll ads targeting real estate keywords
- Google: "wholesaling course" + "how to wholesale real estate" keywords
- Budget: Start at $50-100/day, scale based on CAC vs. LTV

### Launch Sequence
1. **Weeks 1-2:** Build landing page, record lead magnet, set up email sequence
2. **Weeks 3-4:** Create webinar/VSL, test with warm audience (social + email list)
3. **Weeks 5-6:** Soft launch to first cohort (10-20 students, discounted pricing)
4. **Weeks 7-8:** Collect testimonials, refine curriculum based on feedback
5. **Month 3:** Full launch with paid ads, affiliate partnerships, podcast tour

### Affiliate / Referral Program
- 20-30% commission on all referral sales
- Recruit top wholesalers and agents as affiliates
- Provide affiliate swipe copy, emails, and social media assets
